Usage notes

  • Drag and Drop players
  • Click on the player to edit their status

Statuses

  • Active players have text: A
  • Write the number of stones already counted (that the player shows with their fingers)
  • For players down to eight write C as prefix (eg. C6 - counted six stones of eight)
  • For pinned and pinning players append text: P (e.g. 4P - counted three stones and pinned, AP - active player pinning)
